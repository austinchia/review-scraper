**Negative Themes:**

1.  **Challenges with User Acquisition and Conversion (especially for niche products):**
    *   Many founders, despite solving personal pain points or conducting surveys, struggle to convert free users to paying customers, or even acquire free users. This is a significant point of frustration.
    *   Specific issues include low conversion from website clicks to sign-ups, and trial users failing to convert to paid subscriptions due to payment issues (e.g., cards failing at the end of a trial).

2.  **Negative Workplace Culture in Fast-Growth/PE-Backed SaaS Companies:**
